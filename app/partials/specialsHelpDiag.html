Rolling item specials

When rolling items

When a weapon, shield, or armor is rolled, there is a probability for "get special powers and roll again". When Recursive Specials is enabled, another special could be rolled again during the "and roll again" part. Disabling this option will only allow one retrieval of specials per item.

When rolling specials

When rolling for a special power for a weapon, shield, or armor, there is a 1% chance to roll twice and return two specials. When Recursive Specials is enabled, there is a 1% chance for either of those specials to return another two specials. Disabling this option will allow a maximum of only two specials per "get special powers" request.
