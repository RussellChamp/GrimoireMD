Generating a Spellbook

Highest Spell Level

The highest spell level in the spellbook is the highest available to a Wizard with the given Caster Level

Number of Spells

A spellbook will always contain 10 cantrips. It is assumed that each time the spell caster qualifies for a new spell level, the two "free" spells gained from that level and the next level will allocated to that spell level. After that, it is assumed that the spell caster will slowly add additional spells to their spellbook as they progress in level.

Sources

Spells are pulled from sources that have their Spells checkbox marked under Configure Sources. WARNING! If you disable too many sources, the Get Spellbook function may fail and kill your session!
